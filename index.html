About US

Siraj Associates is a construction and contracting firm based in Kochi offering services in civil construction, technical consultancy, project management, and interior and landscaping works. The firm was established by Mr. Mohammed Siraj, a chartered civil engineer with more than 30 years of experience in civil contracting and construction management. After graduating from Govt. Engineering College, Thrissur in 1988, he has to his credit the experience of starting Delta Projects, a civil contracting firm in 1995, and leading the firm till 2001 as the managing partner. Later, when upgraded to Delco Project Pvt. Limited, he was the Executive Director from 2001 to 2011. He then led Faizaan Interiors and Developers as the CEO from 2011 to 2019.

Our experience and expertise lie in civil and finishing works of high-rise buildings, commercial and institutional structures, and residential villas with properties panned in Kerala and Bangalore.

Backed up by an adept team of professionals and headed by Mr. Siraj, we strive to offer services with an uncompromised commitment to Quality, Health, and Safety.
